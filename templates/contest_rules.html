Volcano Judge Contest Rules


Introduction
You are required to follow these rules when writing a Volcano Judge contest. Failure to comply with these rules may result in one or more of the following:

  • A ban from Volcano Judge services
  • A -1000 performance for the contest
  • The contest becoming unrated for you

Before Contest
We expect the following from you before a contest:
  • Wait until the registration date to register for the contest
  • Don't try to find or share contest problems with others

During Contest
We expect the following from you during a contest:
  • The only website you may visit during a contest is: volcanojudge.pythonanywhere.com
  • Don't discuss contest details or problems with anyone
  • Don't share your performance with anyone
  • Don't use previously-saved algorithms

After Contest
We expect the following from you after a contest (until the Ends On date):
  • Don't discuss contest details or problems with anyone
  • Don't share your performance with anyone

These rules are enforced by Volcano Judge staff and monitoring is done by staff as well as the contest manager.
